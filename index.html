THE ORIGIN STORY

NOT APART
FROM NATURE

Most people think conservation happens in boardrooms. For me, it started with a shovel in an oil sand mine in Canada to earn money for a flight to Africa.

Since 2009, I've lived in the Kalahari. It’s hard, lonely, and often frustrating work. But when you walk with a predator that sees you as an equal, the boundaries of your world shift.
